Latitude vs. Maximum Temperature

Cities that are closest to the equator, or 0° degrees Latitude, have the highest max temperatures. Cities in the southern hemishpere (-90° to 0° Latitude) tended to have higher maximum temperatures than cities in the northern hemishpere (0° to 90° Latitude)
